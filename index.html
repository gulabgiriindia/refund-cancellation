Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated on Dec 24th 2023

At GulabGiri, we strive to provide the best service and products to our customers. However, we understand that there might be situations where you need to request a refund or cancel an order. Please read our Refund & Cancellation policy carefully.

Refund Policy:

We offer a refund on products only in the following cases:

  • Defective or damaged products received.

  • Incorrect product shipped.

To initiate a refund, please contact our customer support within 7 days of receiving the product. Provide detailed information and, if possible, attach images of the defective or damaged product.

Cancellation Policy:

You can cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it. Please contact our customer support with your order details for cancellation.

Note:

- Refunds will be processed within 7 business days after approval.
- Cancellation requests beyond 24 hours will not be entertained.

For any further assistance or inquiries, please reach out to our customer support at info.gulabgiri@gmail.com.
